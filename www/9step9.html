Minimum

The initial moisture conditions for construction materials are determined by literature, such as the ASHREA 160 regulations or EN15026.
There, for new constructions, the initial moisture content of materials should be two times EMC90* for concrete and two times EMC80 for all other materials, except when drying or protection procedures are implemented during the construction phase. For existing constructions and retrofit designs, the predefined initial conditions are EMC90 for concrete and EMC80 for all other materials. According to the EN 15026 standard, a temperature of 20 째C and humidity of 80% is applied for existing buildings and 20 째C except for new builds were an humidity of 50% shall be applied, if materials were stored in conditioned places.
